Library Operating Hours

  • Monday–Friday: 9 AM – 10 PM
  • Saturday: 10 AM – 6 PM
  • Sunday: Closed

Library Rules

  • No food or drink allowed inside the library.
  • Mobile phones must be on silent mode at all times.
  • Maintain quietness and respect others.

Borrowing Policies

  • Undergraduate students may borrow up to 5 books at a time for up to 14 days.
  • Postgraduate students: Up to 7 books for 21 days.
  • Books can be renewed once if not requested by another student.
  • Late returns incur a fine of ₹10 per day per book.
